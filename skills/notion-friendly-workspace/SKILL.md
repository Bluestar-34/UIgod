# Notion Friendly Workspace

This child skill captures a Notion-inspired AI workspace campaign style derived from `https://www.notion.com/`.

It is designed for web work that should feel friendly and easy to adopt, while the first impression is still a memorable AI campaign rather than a generic warm SaaS page.

Default representative page type: an AI workspace campaign homepage with a dark hero, large white product window, sticker agents, logo belt, and warm bento product sections. When the user does not specify a page type, generate this one.

## Use When

Use this skill for:

- AI workspace campaign homepages
- productivity-tool launch pages
- friendly automation product sites
- Notion-like app marketing pages
- feature-tour pages that need playful product clarity

## Style Priority

Preserve the two-layer identity:

- dark midnight campaign hero
- friendly warm Notion productivity sections
- sticker-like agents and hand-drawn tracks
- large crisp product window
- compact blue CTAs
- one humanist sans type hierarchy

Framework choice comes after the style logic is understood.

The bundled examples are framework-neutral HTML and CSS so they can be translated into another stack.

Use `DESIGN.md` as the single design truth source. Treat this file as the operational wrapper around it.

## Expected Output

A strong result should feel like a Notion AI campaign page:

- the first viewport is dark, memorable, and product-led
- the product window is the largest visual object
- stickers and blue tracks add friendly motion and agent personality
- lower sections use warm off-white bento cards
- CTAs stay compact, rounded, and blue

## Guardrails

- Do not turn the default output into an all-white generic SaaS homepage.
- Do not drop the dark campaign hero.
- Do not make the product window a vague placeholder block.
- Keep stickers as accents, not decorative clutter.
- Keep one humanist sans hierarchy intact.
